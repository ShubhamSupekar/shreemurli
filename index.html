Why We Use Jaggery and Spices in Our Tea

At Shree Murli, we believe in enriching your tea experience with the natural goodness of jaggery and aromatic spices. Unlike refined sugar, jaggery is unprocessed and retains its natural minerals and vitamins, adding a deep, complex sweetness to your cup. This healthier alternative not only enhances the flavor but also offers a range of nutritional benefits.

Our Unique Spice Blend
